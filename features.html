Features and Learnin'

"But what does Markdown Here actually do?"

Markdown Here is an extension for Chrome, Firefox, Thunderbird, Postbox, and Icedove. With it, you can write email in Markdown, and then click a button (or context menu item, or use a hotkey) to render the email into good-looking, fully formatted text (HTML, technically).
