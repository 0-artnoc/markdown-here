New logo time!


And you can help create it!

Designers: There are about 50,000 Markdown Here users, and it's likely that a lot of them will need design work in the future. Submitting a design could be good for you.

Everyone: In about a month you'll get to vote on the new designs!

Some users suggested that they would like a new logo, so I linked to a poll in the previous release (v2.11.4) asking people to vote on the matter. And... a clear majority think it's time for a new logo! (Thanks to everyone who voted, by the way. I read all of your comments.)

So now I'm going to gather submissions and set up a voting structure, which I'll link to in a subsequent release. So, for right now, it's the design people I need the most. In about a month (early May), when it's time to vote, I'll need everyone else as well.

To submit your logo(s), please email me at pritchard.adam@gmail.com or post to the Markdown Here Google Group.

Requirements/guidelines:

  • You can submit up to three designs. (I guess? I don't know. Submit more if you want, but rank them or something. If there are too many overall I'll have to cut some. But there probably won't be too many.)
  • Submit a 16x16 version and a 512x512 version in PNG format. (They can be simply different sizes of the same image, but often the smaller needs to be simpler than the large.)
    • I'll need a wider array of sizes from the winner, of course. See the current logo sizes.
  • You need to be willing to accept the Contributor License Agreement. (It's not really that onerous. Non-exclusive perpetual license, etc.)
  • Don't worry about the existing purple-and-pink palette. The MDH site and options page can be changed to fit the new logo.
  • I'm vetoing the standard Markdown logo (which I'm not even going to link to). Too generic. Variations on it are okay, but original work, please!

Please feel free to contact me with questions, concerns, etc.

Thanks for participating!

—Adam
